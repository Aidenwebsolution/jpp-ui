About

Jaipur Pink Panthers is a professional kabaddi team based in Jaipur, Rajasthan that competes in the Pro Kabaddi League.

Founded: 2014

Owner: Abhishek Bachchan

Coach: Kasinathan Baskaran

Captain: Navneet Gautam

Home ground: Sawai Mansingh stadium, Jaipur

Team colours: Pink and Blue

Current squad: 15 Indian & 3 International players

Jaipur Pink Panthers are the champions of the inaugural season of the Pro Kabaddi League in 2014
